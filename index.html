Ivana Watson

Esthetician · Developer · Innovator

As a student of Software Development at DeVry University, I am passionate about technology and its potential to shape the future. My academic journey has built a strong foundation in programming, software engineering, and systems design.

I am also the founder of 13 Esthetics — a skincare brand where I blend technical expertise with entrepreneurial vision. This dual path has deepened my skills in project management, e-commerce, and innovative product development, especially as I explore smart anti-aging solutions and LED therapy devices.

Motivated by the intersection of technology and wellness, my long-term vision is to scale 13 Esthetics into a leading beauty tech brand — manufacturing proprietary IoT skincare devices from the ground up.
